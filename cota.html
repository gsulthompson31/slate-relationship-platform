The College of the Arts

At Georgia State, the arts are more than performance — they’re preparation. The College of the Arts helps you build your craft, your voice and your professional edge in the heart of Atlanta’s creative economy.

Create Boldly. Perform Confidently. Build A Career In The Arts.

The College of the Arts is where passion becomes practice — through rigorous training, collaboration and real opportunities to make and share work. You’ll learn in studios, stages and spaces that encourage experimentation while helping you develop the discipline professionals rely on.

With Atlanta as your backdrop, you’ll connect your talent to experience: showcases, productions, exhibitions, internships and partnerships that help you build a portfolio and a network — so you’re ready for what comes next.

  • Professional Training In Your Discipline Faculty mentorship and hands-on practice that strengthen technique, confidence and creative range.
  • Real Stages, Real Audiences Performances and exhibitions that help you learn how to deliver — and how to grow through feedback.
  • Atlanta’s Creative Advantage Access to a major arts and media hub where connections, experience and opportunity are part of the curriculum.
