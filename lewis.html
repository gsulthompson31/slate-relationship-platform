Byrdine F. Lewis College of Nursing and Health Professions

At Georgia State, your college is more than a major — it’s your community, your momentum, and your launchpad into a career that changes lives.

Built for Impact. Backed by Atlanta.

The Byrdine F. Lewis College of Nursing and Health Professions is where purpose meets preparation. You’ll learn alongside faculty and peers who are focused on what matters most: caring for people, strengthening communities, and leading the future of health.

Here, your education is connected to real-world practice — in one of the most dynamic health-care environments in the country. You’ll be supported, challenged, and celebrated as you grow into the kind of professional patients remember and teams rely on.

  • Career-Ready Learning Hands-on training and applied experiences that build confidence — not just credits.
  • A Community That Shows Up for You Advising, coaching, and faculty mentorship designed to keep you progressing and thriving.
  • Purpose You Can Feel Programs rooted in service, equity, and leadership — because health care is human work.
