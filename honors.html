Georgia State Honors College

At Georgia State, Honors is more than an added credential — it’s a close-knit community that helps you think bigger, go deeper, and build a standout path from college to career.

Challenge Yourself. Find Your People. Lead With Purpose.

The Honors College is designed for students who want more from their Georgia State experience — more meaningful learning, more faculty connection, and more opportunities to turn curiosity into real-world impact.

Through small, discussion-based courses, research and creative work, and high-touch advising, you’ll build the skills that matter: critical thinking, communication, initiative, and leadership. It’s a powerful advantage — in Atlanta and anywhere your ambition takes you.

  • Small Classes, Big Conversations Seminar-style learning that sharpens how you think, write, and lead — with faculty who know your name.
  • Research And Real Experience Opportunities to explore, create, and contribute — building a resume that tells a compelling story.
  • Advising That Moves You Forward Personalized guidance for majors, internships, graduate school, and nationally competitive opportunities.
